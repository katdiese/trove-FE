What is Trove?

Trove is a discovery and communication tool for farmers market patrons and vendors. Whether you’re at home planning your day, or you’re wandering through your favorite Farmer’s Market, Trove is there for you.

Discovery

Looking for a new farmers market? Need info on an old standby? Trove is the tool for you. With information on farmers markets all across the United States there’s a treasure trove of information and possibility out there.

Communication

With realtime chat and user images you’ll always know what’s going on. Patrons can ask questions and share their finds. Vendors can answer questions and post deals to strengthen their connection with their customers.
